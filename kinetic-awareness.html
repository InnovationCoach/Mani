Kinetic Awareness — Moving Art for Change

A 9-week program where students explore the physics of motion, culminating in an art event to raise environmental awareness and build interactive kinetic sculptures.

Duration

9 Weeks

Core Focus

Physics in Motion

Final Outcome

Kinetic Art Event

Program Philosophy

This isn’t just another checklist—it’s a personal power-up guide for becoming a stronger thinker, collaborator, and change-maker. In this project, students don’t just complete tasks; they become strategic thinkers, innovative designers, and culturally aware, adaptable leaders.

The skills developed aren’t just for this project — they’re life skills that will make students future-ready for any challenge, career, or collaboration.

The Learning Journey

Phase 1: Exploration (Weeks 1-4)

Learners choose and explore the physics of motion through hands-on activities. They will learn how to use the scientific process as a tool to gain a better understanding of the connection between Mathematics and the principles of motion in Physics.

Phase 2: Creation & Awareness (Weeks 5-9)

Students get together and organize an event to raise awareness about an environmental issue. Each group of learners uses their understanding to build an interactive prototype, demonstrating their concerns through a fusion of art, physics, and creativity.

Core Competencies Developed

Strategic Thinking

Planning, goal setting, and organizing ideas.

Analytical Thinking

Interpreting data and making logical conclusions.

Engineering Design

Using the design process to create, test, and improve solutions.
